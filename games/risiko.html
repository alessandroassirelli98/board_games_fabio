Regole: Risiko

Obiettivo

Devi ammazzare tutti

Giocatori

Da 2 a 4

Setup

  1. Distribute resources evenly among players.
  2. Shuffle and place the deck of cards at the center.
  3. Each player chooses a starting position on the board.

Gameplay

  • On your turn, roll the dice and move your token.
  • Perform actions based on the tile you land on.
  • Trade resources with other players to maximize your strategy.
Remember: You need 10 points to win the game. Plan your moves wisely!
